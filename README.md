## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- **Windows 10 or 11** (64-bit).
- **League of Legends** - a valid game installation.

### Installation

1. Download from **[divineskins.gg](https://divineskins.gg/install-mods)** or grab the [latest release](https://github.com/DivineSkins/celestial-releases/releases/latest).
2. Pick your installer:
   - `Celestial_<version>_x64-setup.exe` - consumer NSIS installer (recommended)
   - `Celestial_<version>_x64_en-US.msi` - MSI installer
3. Run the installer and launch **Celestial Launcher**.


### Installing Mods

1. Browse skins on [divineskins.gg](https://divineskins.gg/explore-mods) or import a `.modpkg` / `.fantome` file directly into the launcher.
2. Enable the mods you want in your library, then click **Start patcher** to apply.
3. Launch League of Legends - your selected mods are active until you click **Stop** in the launcher.
